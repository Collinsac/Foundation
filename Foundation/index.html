Our Purpose

We are established on three pillars

Translation of Innovation

Conduct and promote translation of innovation in the field of food, nutrition and health.

Validated Solutions for the Scientific Community

Provide validated innovative solutions for the scientific community with a view to leverage research in the field of food, nutrition and health.

Publication and Dissemination of Scientific Evidence

Contribute directly or indirectly to the publication and dissemination of scientific evidence in the field of food, nutrition and health.

Our Activities

We work in the translation of state of the art technological prototypes towards delivering value-added innovative solutions for researchers, healthcare professionals and patients

Technology translation

We conduct in-house product development according to industry standards; technology and solution validation; and product certification.

Counselling services

We offer quality and regulatory counselling services for researchers and academic institutions running clinical trials in the field of nutrition and health, to ensure well-designed and compliant trail designs.

International Focus

Our impact has international reach

We operate from Switzerland, and are based at Biopôle, a life sciences Campus in Lausanne bringing together a strong community of +100 life sciences companies and +25 research groups.
